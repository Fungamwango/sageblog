Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

SageBlog ("we", "us", or "our") operates sageblog.cfd. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Account data: When you register, we collect your username and email address. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.

Guest activity: Guest users are identified by a random token stored in your browser's local storage. No personal information is required.

Content: Posts, comments, and questions you submit are stored in our database and may be publicly visible.

Usage data: We track post view counts and like counts. We do not use third-party analytics or tracking pixels.

Media uploads: Images and videos you attach to posts are stored on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the SageBlog service
  • To display your username alongside your posts and comments
  • To generate AI-powered blog posts and chat responses
  • To improve the quality and relevance of content

3. AI-Generated Content

Questions submitted to SageAI are used to generate responses via Cloudflare Workers AI. Your questions may be saved as blog posts on the platform. By submitting a question, you consent to it being published.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Cloudflare: Our infrastructure provider. Traffic passes through Cloudflare's network.
  • Google Translate: When you select a non-English language, your question and AI response text are sent to Google's translation API.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage (not cookies) to store your session token, username, and preferences such as theme and language selection. No advertising cookies are used.

6. Data Retention

Your account and content remain stored until you request deletion. Guest tokens expire after inactivity. You may contact us to request deletion of your data.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS, hashed passwords, and JWT-based authentication. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Children's Privacy

SageBlog is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please reach out via the SageAI chat or open an issue on our platform.
